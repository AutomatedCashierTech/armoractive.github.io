Mission

ArmorActive specializes in manufacturing and supporting purpose-built solutions for deploying tablet computers into any business landscape.

Our quest is to discover and build ways to make the world more simple, more convenient, more engaging, more beautiful. Our roots are deep in inventing and reinventing our products, and our industry. We don’t sit by and watch competitors try first instead we blaze new trails. We are agile and nimble, and evolve as we grow.

Vision

The “Armor” in our name stands for our knowledge in developing secure hardware for tablet computing. We specialize in building solutions and protecting tablet devices. The “Active” in our name stands for the services and software we create for the same devices we protect. ArmorActive makes it possible to place tablet devices anywhere imaginable.

Values

  • Service: Our greatest investments are in the services we provide to our customers.
