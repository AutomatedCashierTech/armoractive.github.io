Resembling our best selling iPad enclosure, the Full Metal Jacket, the Full Metal Swap is a secure enclosure with a quick entry key-locking system. But where this product delivers is in its ability to secure several different models of Android tablets. By using custom inserts each tablet is stabilized and secured by the enclosure. Custom branding options are availible to make this enclosure unique and your own. See our custom etching page for details.

The enclosure will accommodate the dimensions of the following tablets; Asus EEE Pad 10.1, Asus EEE Pad Transformer Prime, Acer Iconia A200, Acer Iconia A500, Acer Iconia W500, Asus Vivo, Dell Lattitude, Lenovo ThinkPad (2), and Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1 *Buttons and/or cable ports on certain tablets may require modification to the enclosure and/or cables for complete compatibility.
