The Wedge tablet wall mount offers an ADA wall mount solution for those requiring compliance with accesibility regulations. Unique to this wall mount is the design for mounting to standard dual outlet electrical boxes. The small concealed area between the wall and enclosure allow for securely installing a power adapter and cable to enable continuous power to the tablet device.
